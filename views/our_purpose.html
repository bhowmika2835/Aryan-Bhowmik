Our Purpose

There are many endangered species out there and we think that this initiative will allow the animal population to sustain itself. Our goal is for zoos to be able to use this network to find mates for their animals so that they can further their development. You can think of this as somewhat of an animal tinder and it is up to the zoos to pick out the right mate. This software could be a game-changer and we think that it could really bloom all across the world in the future.
